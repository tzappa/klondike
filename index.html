About

Klondike Solitaire is a classic card game that is played with a standard 52-card deck. The game is won when all cards are moved to the four Foundation piles.

Rules

Goal: Move all cards to the four Foundation piles, starting with the Ace and ending with the King in each suit.

Tableau: Build down in alternating colors. The top card of each pile is available for play on the Foundations or another Tableau pile. Empty Tableau piles may be filled with a King or a pile starting with a King.

Stock: Click to deal one card. The Stock can be re-dealt as many times as needed.

Waste: Cards from the Stock are dealt to the Waste pile. Cards in the Waste pile are available for play on the Foundations or Tableau piles.

Foundation: Build up in suit from Ace to King.

Controls

Click a card to select it, click on a pile to move selected card. Double-click a card to move it to the Foundation.

Click on 'Auto Move' button to automatically move cards to the Foundation.
